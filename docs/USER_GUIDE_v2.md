# Foodiq 2.0 — User Guide

What’s new for customers after the Version 2.0 release. The look and feel of Foodiq is unchanged.

## Rate a delivered order

1. Open **My Orders**.
2. On a **Delivered** order, tap **Rate Order** (or open order details).
3. Scroll to **Rate your order**.
4. Star the **restaurant**, **delivery partner** (if assigned), and **overall** experience.
5. Optionally add comments, then submit.

You can rate each order only once.

## Help & Support

Open **Help & Support** from the menu.

| Mode | Use when |
|------|----------|
| **Support ticket** | Order, payment, or account issues |
| **Report a bug** | Something is broken (page error, wrong data) |
| **Product feedback** | Ideas or general suggestions |

We also keep FAQs and contact info on the same page.

## What did not change

- Browse restaurants, cart, checkout, Razorpay payments
- Live order tracking
- Profile, addresses, coupons, favorites
- Login / register / OTP flows

## Privacy

Ratings and feedback are tied to your account when you are signed in. Bug reports may include the page URL and a short description you provide.

## Need help?

Use Help & Support → Support ticket, or the contact options listed there.
